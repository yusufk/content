Ingredients:  
  
2 cups flour  
2cups b/p  
Pinch of salt  
½ cup milk  
¼ cup sugar  
2 oz butter (60g)  
1 egg  
1 tsp vanilla essence  
  
  
Method:  
Cream butter-sugar method.  
Than roll 5-6 flat round cakes.  
Bake in oven.  
  
Custard:  
1L milk 4 tblspn (level) custard sugar + condensed milk.
